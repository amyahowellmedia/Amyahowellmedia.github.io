From Grit-and-Grind to Analytics-Driven: How the Celtics Reinvented Winning

Using two decades of Boston Celtics data — from the Paul Pierce–Kevin Garnett years to the Jayson Tatum–Jaylen Brown era — this project tracks how shifts in coaching, pace, and offensive and defensive efficiency transformed the team from a defense-first group under Doc Rivers into a faster, analytics-driven contender under Joe Mazzulla, revealing how one franchise’s evolution mirrors the modern NBA.

Amya Howell
